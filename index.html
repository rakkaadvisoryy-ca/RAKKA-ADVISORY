Pay with M-Pesa (STK Push)

Enter your number, confirm the prompt on your phone, and you’ll receive payment confirmation on-screen.

You will receive a Safaricom prompt on your phone. Confirm and enter your M-Pesa PIN to complete.

What happens after you click Pay?

  1. We send a secure request to Safaricom (Daraja).
  2. You receive a payment prompt on your phone.
  3. You confirm and enter your PIN.
  4. Your payment is recorded and we proceed with your service.

If you don’t see a prompt, ensure your number is correct and your phone has network.
